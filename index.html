About S' thetíx

S' thetíx is a Japanese-German company specializing in the development and production of aesthetic medical devices for the health and beauty industry. Our team includes doctors, engineers, and technicians with extensive experience in creating innovative solutions.

We are committed to offering the most advanced technologies and customized solutions for our clients. We develop 100% of our products at our central facilities in Japan & Germany and are present in over 15 countries.
